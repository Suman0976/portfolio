Professional Experience

Mrs. Suman Kaushik
Principal | Bal Bharti Vidya Peeth Sr. Secondary School, Gohana, Haryana

Mrs. Suman Kaushik is an educational leader with over a decade of experience in transforming schools and shaping young minds. Starting as a teacher and progressing to Senior Lecturer, her dedication to student development earned her the position of Principal. She is committed to providing students with opportunities for academic and personal growth, fostering a learning environment where every child can thrive.
With an MA in Economics, an M.Ed. , and advanced training in school administration and leadership, Mrs. Suman continually refines her skills through professional development programs. Her innovative approach has led to significant improvements in student engagement and academic outcomes at Bal Bharti Vidya Peeth Sr. Secondary School.

Vision and Leadership

Mrs. Suman believes in nurturing responsible, self-reliant citizens equipped with 21st-century skills. Under her leadership, the school has seen notable growth in academic performance, cultural activities, and environmental initiatives. She fosters a collaborative environment where students, teachers, and parents work together to create a positive, inclusive school culture. She believes in empowering her staff through professional development, ensuring they remain at the forefront of educational innovation.

Achievements and Impact

Under Mrs. Suman’s leadership, Bal Bharti Vidya Peeth has achieved milestones in both academics and extracurricular activities. Her meticulous planning and ability to adapt to changing educational needs have led to increased student enrollment and recognition at national platforms. By integrating sustainable practices into the curriculum, she has encouraged students to become environmentally conscious citizens.

Her accomplishments are further reflected in the prestigious awards she has received, including:

  • Vivekananda Excellence Award
  • Avantika Shikshak Samman
  • Paryavaran Dronacharya Award
  • Best Teacher Award
  • Recognition from Bharat Vikas Parishad for National Programs
  • Best Cultural Coordinator Award

Workshops and Initiatives

Mrs. Suman has conducted and attended various workshops for teachers and students on topics such as:

  • Time Management and Prioritization
  • Conflict Resolution
  • Student Personality Development
  • Environmental Awareness
  • Value-Based Education Practices

Empowering the Future

In addition to supporting her students, Mrs. Suman is also deeply invested in the professional growth of her staff. She believes in creating an environment where teachers and staff feel valued and motivated, offering them opportunities for professional development and empowering them to succeed in their roles. As a strong advocate for gender equality, Mrs. Suman is especially committed to the empowerment of girls through education. She has made it a priority to ensure that young girls in the school receive the support and resources they need to succeed, helping them break barriers and pursue their dreams confidently. Above all, Mrs. Suman believes in creating future leaders. Her focus is not only on academic excellence but also on fostering qualities such as responsibility, resilience, and compassion. By preparing students with the skills and mindset needed to succeed in life, she is shaping the next generation of leaders who will make a positive impact in the world.

"Every child is born with a unique potential; as an educational leader, my mission is to provide the tools, guidance, and opportunities they need to discover their purpose and shine in their own extraordinary way.”
- Mrs. Suman Kaushik
